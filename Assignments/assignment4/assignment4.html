As the sample size increases, the distribution of sample means will concentrate around the population mean due to the Central Limit Theorem. As the number of simulations increases, the distribution of sample means will more accurately reflect the population mean (higher precision)

num_simulations = 2000
sample_size     = 100

vec_xbar = [None] * num_simulations

for iteration in range(num_simulations):
    vec_unif  = np.random.uniform(low = -2, high=2, size = sample_size)
    vec_xbar[iteration] = vec_unif.mean()
